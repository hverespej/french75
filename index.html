French 75 Recipe



Ingredients
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 11 ounce gin
  • 11/2 ounce lemon juice
  • 11/2 ounce simple syrup
  • 14 ounces champagne

Instructions
  1. Put ice cubes in cocktail shaker
  2. Pour gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup over ice
  3. Shake vigorously
  4. Strain into a chilled champagne flute
  5. Top with champagne
  6. Garnish with lemon twist
